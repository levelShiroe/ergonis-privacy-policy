# Privacy Policy for Ergonis

**Last updated:** June 18, 2026

Ergonis is a productivity and quest-management app operated by Eternal Games.

This Privacy Policy explains what information Ergonis collects, how it is used, and how users can request deletion of their account and data.

## Information We Collect

Ergonis may collect and store the following information:

- **Account information:** Google Sign-In information such as your email address, display name, and Firebase user ID.
- **App profile information:** character name, selected class, level, experience points, friend code, and public profile details used for friend discovery.
- **Quest and progress data:** quests you create, quest categories, quest descriptions, completion progress, completion history, and related app activity.
- **Friend and Guild data:** friend requests, friends list, quest giver access settings, sent quests, received quests, and quest responses.
- **Notification data:** device notification tokens, if notifications are enabled.
- **Support information:** information you choose to provide when contacting support.

Ergonis does not sell your personal data.

## How We Use Information

We use the information collected to:

- Provide Google Sign-In and account access.
- Save and sync your quests, progress, character profile, and Guild/Friends features.
- Allow friend discovery through friend codes.
- Send or receive friend quests when users choose to use those features.
- Provide app notifications when enabled.
- Improve reliability, troubleshoot issues, and respond to support requests.

## Data Sharing

Ergonis uses Firebase and Google services to provide authentication, cloud storage, synchronization, and notifications.

Some profile information may be visible to other Ergonis users when you use friend features. This may include your display name, friend code, selected class, level, and friend/quest interaction status.

We do not sell user data to advertisers or data brokers.

## Data Storage and Security

Ergonis stores app data locally on your device and may sync data to Firebase/Firestore cloud services.

Data transmitted between the app and Firebase/Google services is protected using encrypted connections. No method of transmission or storage is completely secure, but we use reasonable safeguards to protect user data.

## Account and Data Deletion

Users may request deletion of their Ergonis account and associated app data by contacting:

**eternalgames17@gmail.com**

Please include the subject:

**Ergonis Account Deletion Request**

In your request, include the Google account email used with Ergonis or your Ergonis friend code so we can identify the correct account.

When an account deletion request is processed, Ergonis account data stored in Firebase/Firestore will be deleted, including profile data, quest data, progress data, friend data, and related app records where reasonably possible.

Some information may be retained temporarily if required for security, fraud prevention, legal compliance, or backup recovery.

## Children

Ergonis is not intended for children under 13. The app is intended for users age 13 and older.

## Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be posted on this page with a new “Last updated” date.
