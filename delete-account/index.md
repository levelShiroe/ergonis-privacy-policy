# Ergonis Account and Data Deletion

**Last updated:** June 18, 2026

This page explains how Ergonis users can request deletion of their account and associated app data.

Ergonis is operated by Eternal Games.

## Request Account Deletion

To request deletion of your Ergonis account and associated app data, email:

**ergonisofficial@proton.me**

Use the subject line:

**Ergonis Account Deletion Request**

In your request, include one of the following so we can identify the correct account:

- The Google account email used to sign in to Ergonis
- Your Ergonis friend code, if available

## Data That Will Be Deleted

When your account deletion request is processed, the following Ergonis data will be deleted where reasonably possible:

- Account profile data
- Character/profile data
- Friend code and public profile data
- Quest data
- Quest progress
- Completion history
- Friend requests
- Friends list
- Sent and received friend quests
- Notification token data associated with your account

## Data That May Be Kept Temporarily

Some data may be kept temporarily if needed for:

- Security
- Fraud prevention
- Legal compliance
- Backup recovery
- Resolving active support requests

## In-App Deletion

Ergonis may also provide an in-app account deletion or deletion request option from the app’s settings/account area.

## Contact

For privacy questions or account deletion requests, contact:

**ergonisofficial@proton.me**
